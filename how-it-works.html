Common Questions

How long does a scan take? +
Small repositories typically scan in under 30 seconds. Large monorepos with thousands of files may take 2-5 minutes. Scans run asynchronously so they never block your dashboard.
Is my source code ever sent to Scannify's servers? +
In Cloud mode, the scanning worker clones your repository temporarily to run Trivy and Gitleaks. In Agent mode, scanning happens entirely on your own infrastructure ‚Äî your code never leaves your network.
How are my GitHub/GitLab tokens protected? +
Every token is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being stored in the database. The encryption key is stored separately from the database. Even if the database were compromised, the tokens would be unreadable without the key.
Can multiple users share one git connection? +
Yes. Once an admin connects a GitHub/GitLab/Bitbucket account, all users within the same company can browse and scan those repositories without needing their own tokens.
What happens when my trial expires? +
Your account moves to EXPIRED status and access is paused. Your data is retained. The platform owner can extend your trial or activate a paid plan at any time.
What programming languages are supported? +
Trivy supports all major ecosystems: Node.js/npm, Python/pip, Go modules, Java/Maven/Gradle, Ruby/Gem, PHP/Composer, .NET/NuGet, Rust/Cargo, and more.
