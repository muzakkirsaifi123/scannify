What is Agent Mode?

Agent Mode lets you run the Scannify scanner on your own infrastructure. You get a unique API key, deploy our lightweight scanner container, and it reports back to your dashboard ‚Äî giving you full control over where your code is analyzed and saving 50% on your subscription.

1 Toggle Agent Mode in your dashboard
2 Copy your unique Agent API Key
3 Run our Docker container on your infra
4 Results sync to your Scannify dashboard

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial? +
Yes ‚Äî every plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to all features of your chosen plan during the trial period.
Can I switch between Cloud and Agent mode? +
Absolutely. You can toggle between Cloud and Agent mode from your dashboard settings at any time. Switching to Agent mode immediately applies the 50% discount on your next billing cycle. Your Agent API key is generated automatically.
What counts as a "user" in my plan? +
A user is any person with a login to your company workspace in Scannify Hub ‚Äî including admins, developers, and read-only viewers. Service accounts and automated integrations do not count toward your user limit.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? +
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No cancellation fees.
Is my code or token data stored on your servers? +
In Cloud Mode, scan jobs run on our secure infrastructure and results are stored encrypted. Your source code is never stored ‚Äî only scan metadata and findings. All access tokens are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being saved. In Agent Mode, your code never leaves your own infrastructure.
Do you offer non-profit or open-source discounts? +
Yes ‚Äî verified non-profits and open-source projects with public repositories can apply for our community program, which includes a significantly reduced rate or free access to the Small Team plan. Contact us with details about your project.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay by wire transfer or invoice on net-30 terms.
